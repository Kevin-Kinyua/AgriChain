WELCOME TO AGRI - CHAIN



Who we are

We are a legally registered national association of growers, exporters and other value-chain players in Kenya. We also welcome international individual and corporate memberships.



What We Do

Working in partnership with public and private stakeholders, to provide innovative value-chain services that influence the transformation of agriculture, empower members and specifically small- holders to provide provenance in response to market demands for improved livelihoods.
